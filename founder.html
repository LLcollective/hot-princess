Case snapshots

Corrective action plan · GME visa documentation

Context. An internal review showed documents were confirmed but not uploaded, creating compliance risk.

What I did. Coordinated stakeholder notification, set a 48 hour upload policy, added system alerts in the platform, delivered short trainings, and set monthly checks with a small quarterly audit.

Outcome. Files were brought current within a few weeks. Delays decreased. No documentation issues were flagged in pre review.

Resident curriculum refresh · ACGME alignment

Context. Training content was dated and uneven across cohorts.

What I did. Ran a quick needs assessment, co designed updated modules with case discussions and compliance elements, and added simple competency checks.

Outcome. Residents were better prepared for rotations and faculty engagement improved. Materials aligned with current ACGME expectations.

Community health initiative · program management

Context. Insurance exits created coverage gaps that limited access to essential services.

What I did. Ran a needs assessment, developed a logic model and grant proposal, coordinated partners, and planned health fairs and mobile clinics with educational workshops. Set clear workflows and trained staff.

Outcome. Access improved through screenings and enrollment support. Funding was secured to sustain the work. The approach was reused on later efforts.
