Meet the Founder

After years working in hospitals, nonprofits, and emergency federal response, I’ve seen firsthand how resilience depends on more than strategy alone; it thrives when clarity and compassion move together. Lace & Lime™ grew out of that truth.

Today, my work blends public health roots with community-driven clarity. The goal is simple: to make wellness and empowerment accessible, steady, and sustainable. This isn’t about quick fixes, it’s about creating rhythms and tools that help people pause, realign, and grow with balance.
