Meet the Founder

Hi, I’m Sharon.

After years working in hospitals, nonprofits, and federal emergency response, I saw firsthand that resilience requires more than strategy alone. It requires systems that integrate clarity with compassion.

Lace & Lime™ grew out of that truth. It is an approach to wellness and empowerment where structure meets intuition and growth is designed to be both grounded and sustainable. At its core, it is about creating rhythms and tools that support alignment, clarity, and a life that feels attainable.

I have never measured my career by titles. My focus has been on acquiring and applying the tools and methods that create measurable impact where they are needed most. Along the way, I have developed a systems-based lens for identifying waste, streamlining platforms, and improving organizational efficiency.

That expertise comes from compliance and accreditation roles, including serving as a public health advisor in federal response, an accreditation manager for post-graduate medical education, and a nonprofit program manager. It is also grounded in my graduate capstone on systems and reimbursements, where I analyzed policy gaps and proposed real-world recommendations. These experiences taught me how to design systems that meet standards and strengthen financial sustainability while creating space for clarity, education, and community wellness.
