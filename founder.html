Founder

Hi, I’m Sharon.

After years working in hospitals, nonprofits, and emergency federal response, I saw that resilience depends on more than strategy alone. It thrives when clarity and compassion move together.

Lace & Lime™ grew out of that truth. It’s an approach to wellness and empowerment where structure meets intuition, and where personal growth feels both grounded and sustainable. At its core, it’s about creating rhythms and tools that support alignment, clarity, and a life that feels attainable.

I help teams go from stuck to shipping in about two weeks by using small wins that compound. As a public health educator and operations strategist, I design tools that make progress feel measurable and doable.
