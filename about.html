About Lace & Lime™

Balance-forward wellness and empowerment bridging NYC and suburban Long Island communities.

Our Story

Lace & Lime™ began with the vision of creating a bridge—between clarity and action, reflection and empowerment, softness and strength. Grounded in public health and lived community experience, we provide tools and spaces for people to reconnect with their own momentum.

Mission

We support under-engaged communities with accessible wellness education, empowerment frameworks, and civic awareness rooted in trust. By honoring both stillness and action, Lace & Lime™ helps individuals and groups move forward with clarity and conviction.

What We Build

Evidence-informed prompts, rituals, and music-driven sets that translate insight into practice—so awareness becomes a sustainable way of moving through the world.

Who It’s For

Under-engaged communities, and anyone ready to step into momentum with presence—NYC, Long Island, and beyond.
