ABOUT

Our story.

Balance-forward tools where clarity meets action, that’s Lace & Lime™.

Start the Journal Explore the Approach

Purpose

To make wellness and empowerment accessible, clear, and actionable. Lace & Lime™ exists to help people move from awareness to aligned action in ways that last.

Foundation

Born from lived experience in healthcare, nonprofits, and emergency response, Lace & Lime™ reflects the truth that resilience is strongest when clarity and compassion work together.

Principles

Wellness and strategy support each other; small, steady shifts create sustainable change; knowledge and tools are shared with clarity and intention.

Method

We design evidence-informed prompts, guided practices, and tools that invite both reflection and action. Each resource is structured to help people pause, realign, and then move forward with confidence.

Workshops

Through tailored sessions and strategic partnerships, we deliver experiences that help people reflect, reset, and strengthen habits they can carry into everyday life.

Tools & Resources

Lace & Lime™ continues to build guided resources, including a forthcoming journal, designed to support reflection, alignment, and sustainable momentum.
