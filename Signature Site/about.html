Our Mission

Empowering individuals and communities with tools, guided reflection, and actionable plans that turn awareness into lasting change.

What We Do

Lace & Lime™ LLC is a wellness and empowerment initiative rooted in Long Island, NY. We create guided journals, resources, and community-centered workshops that help people move from reflection to action with confidence and clarity.

Our Vibe

Founded and led by Sharon, Lace & Lime™ began as a vision to make wellness and empowerment more accessible across Long Island and beyond. Balance-forward, intentional, and rooted in community, we believe growth happens where clarity meets action — and where people feel both grounded and uplifted.
